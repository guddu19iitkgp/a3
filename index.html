Dal


This is a traditional Indian food. In fact, this is a staple food for most Indians. It's just pulses cooked in different ways.This is available in different forms,basically the different dals. 1)Masoor Dal 2)Moong Dal 3)Arhar Dal 4)Sambar Dal 5)Tadka Dal 6).These are generally not spicy and is a good source of protein and very healthy as well.

Paneer


Paneer is a milk product and is consumed by most vegetarians. This can be cooked in various ways. 1)Panneer Butter Masala- This is a traditional Indian recipe cooked using butter and traditional Indian spices. 2)Chilli Paneer- This is a Chinese recipe, basically we add a variety of sauces and cook the paneer with some onions, capcicum and it is a bit more spicy. Paneer is also a good source of protein.
